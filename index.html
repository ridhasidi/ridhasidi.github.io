Oh hi everyone! Welcome to my website!
*Please proceed to Resume page to read my professional resume and experiences.

My name is Ridha and I am really passionate about the environment, the earth system, and GIS. As an avid gamer when I was a child, I was really into computers and video games and I thought I would be a computer engineer when I grew up. However, the 2004 Sumatra–Andaman earthquake and tsunami shifted my interest into earth science.

Finally, I chose to pursue a degree in Geological Engineering. While pursuing my degree, I was introduced to Geographical Information System (GIS) and remote sensing which combine 2 things that I love: technology and earth science. I was captivated by the power of GIS and remote sensing to analyse geospatial data ever since.

I am keen to learn new things, especially languages. Beside my native language — Indonesian, I also can speak English and French fluently. I learned basic Japanese in high school and I am still learning Spanish to this day. I always keep my earphone around, so I can listen to the music anywhere at anytime. Beside hiking, I also love enjoying my Indonesian jasmine tea in the morning while re-watching Eurovision during the weekend.

Thank you very much for your time reading this!
